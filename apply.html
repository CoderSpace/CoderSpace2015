Summer 2016

In our intensive 8-week program students learn the basic coding languages that power the web (HTML, CSS and JS). Through hands on training, CoderSpace youth work in teams to design and build websites for local clients while gaining real world experience in web development, teamwork and leadership.

In addition to providing coding instruction, CoderSpace hosts weekly business trips to local tech companies that expose youth to opportunities in the technology sector. The summer culminates in a Demo Day where student teams show off their client projects to family, friends and supporters.
