Who We Are

CoderSpace is a social enterprise that creates engaging learning experiences to inspire a new generation of leaders and innovators. CoderSpace youth learn how to code, build websites for clients in their communities and gain valuable skills for life and leadership in a digital world.

What We Do

We teach. CoderSpace instructors design and facilitate team-based, project-based activities that create an environment for collaborative and contextual learning.

We build. CoderSpace youth work in teams to design and build websites for local clients while gaining real world experience in web development, teamwork and leadership.

How We Do It

Values. CoderSpace is a community of practice powered by collaboration, curiosity and creativity.

Teams. We work together to share our knowledge, skills and ideas. At CoderSpace, teamwork makes the dream work.

Programs

Summer

In our intensive 8-week program students learn the basic coding languages that power the web while working in teams to build websites for local clients.

Saturdays

During the academic year CoderSpace hosts Saturday meetups for Chicago teens who are learning how to code and working on projects.
