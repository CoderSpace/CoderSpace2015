Saturday Meetups

CoderSpace is a social enterprise that creates engaging learning experiences to inspire a new generation of leaders and innovators. CoderSpace youth learn how to code, build websites for clients in their communities and gain valuable skills for life and leadership in a digital world.

During the academic year CoderSpace hosts Saturday meetups for Chicago teens who are learning how to code and working on projects.

Schedule

CoderSpace meets 10am-4pm every second Saturday during the academic year:

  • October 10
  • November 14
  • December 12
  • January 9
  • February 13
  • March 12
  • April 9
  • May 14
  • June 11

Eligibility

High school students are eligible to join CoderSpace. Priority will be given to students who live in the following community areas:

  • Douglas
  • Englewood
  • Grand Boulevard
  • Greater Grand Crossing
  • Hyde Park
  • Kenwood
  • Oakland
  • South Shore
  • Washington Park
  • West Englewood
  • Woodlawn
